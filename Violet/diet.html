Hormones & Your Body

What you eat plays a major role in regulating hormones, easing PMS, reducing period cramps, improving skin, and stabilizing energy levels throughout your cycle.

Essential Nutrients for Women

  • Iron: Replenishes blood loss during periods (leafy greens, lentils)
  • Omega-3s: Reduces cramps, supports brain function (chia seeds, walnuts, fish)
  • Magnesium: Calms PMS symptoms, supports sleep (dark chocolate, seeds)
  • Calcium + Vitamin D: Supports bones and mood (dairy, tofu, sunlight)
  • Fiber: Balances estrogen and improves gut health (fruits, oats, veggies)

Tips for a Hormone-Friendly Diet

  • Eat regularly and don’t skip meals
  • Balance carbs with proteins & fats
  • Hydrate—drink at least 2 liters of water daily
  • Minimize refined sugar and caffeine close to periods
