Hormones & Your Body

Ever wondered why your mood swings, skin breaks out, or periods go off-track? Let’s decode how estrogen, progesterone, and testosterone shape your body, energy, and emotions.

What Are Hormones?

Hormones are chemical messengers that regulate everything from your mood and metabolism to menstrual cycles and skin. Estrogen, progesterone, and testosterone each play a unique role in maintaining balance in the female body.

Common Symptoms of Hormonal Imbalance

  • Irregular periods or heavy flow
  • Mood swings, anxiety, or depression
  • Sudden weight changes
  • Acne or dry skin
  • Fatigue or sleep issues

When Should You Talk to a Doctor?

While some changes are natural, frequent disruptions in your cycle, extreme mood changes, or prolonged fatigue might be signs of an underlying hormonal issue like PCOS or thyroid imbalance.
