Hormones & Your Body

Your skin reflects your inner health. Discover how hormones, hygiene, and care routines influence skin health, acne, dryness, and glow.

How Hormones Affect Skin

Estrogen keeps your skin supple and hydrated, while testosterone boosts oil production. A hormonal imbalance can lead to acne, dryness, or dull skin tone.

Skincare Tips

  • Wash your face with a gentle cleanser twice daily
  • Use non-comedogenic moisturizers and sunscreen
  • Avoid touching your face frequently
  • Stay hydrated and eat antioxidant-rich foods

When to Seek Help

Persistent breakouts, eczema, or sudden skin changes may signal hormonal issues like PCOS or dermatitis. Consulting a dermatologist can help tailor your care.
