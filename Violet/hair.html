Hormones & Your Body

Your hair reflects inner health and hormone balance. Learn how estrogen, stress, and nutrition affect hair growth, texture, and fall patterns.

Why Hormones Matter for Hair

Estrogen promotes hair growth and thickness, while excess testosterone (as in PCOS) may cause thinning or unwanted facial/body hair. Thyroid hormones also play a role.

Hair Care Essentials

  • Use sulfate-free shampoos and gentle conditioners
  • Avoid excessive heat styling or tight hairstyles
  • Massage your scalp to boost blood flow
  • Eat protein-rich, iron-filled foods for hair strength

When Hair Loss Isn’t Normal

If you notice sudden thinning, bald patches, or clumps of hair falling out, consult a doctor to rule out thyroid issues, PCOS, or alopecia.
